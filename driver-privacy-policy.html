    4. Device data: IP address; device identifier, type and model; mobile operating system; browser or mobile application details;

Phone conversations with our driver support service, as well as with the users of our FASTA app. This is to work with disputes and legal claims, as well as to control the quality of the Service for Driver provided to you. The recordings are kept for a strictly limited time and are available only to persons authorized by us.
